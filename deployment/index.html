Instructions

Voltorb Freedom! is easy to use. To start:

  1. Set the Voltorb counts and coin sums given to you by the puzzle.
  2. Press Solve!
While the solver is running...
  • Inspect the chances that a given cell contains either a coin or Voltorb by clicking on the "Chance" tab. Every time the solver finds a new feasible solution, it will update the cell probabilities.
  • View feasible answers to the puzzle in the "Solutions" tab. Maybe you see a pattern the solver doesn't and can make a choice before it finishes.
  • If you figure out some cells while the solver is running, you can hit "Stop!", flip the cells in the "Input" tab and start it up again. This will narrow down the feasible answers the solver needs to look for and give you more specific suggestions when it finishes.
  • If the solver figures out some cells while it is running, they will be updated in the "Input" tab. No need to wait until the end to keep playing!
  • See how close the solver is to finishing in the progress bar below the puzzle grid.
After the solver finishes
  • Voltorb Flip! will make suggestions for your next move in the "Suggestions and Messages" box. It's up to you to decide which cell you'll flip next.
  • Flip one or more cells in Pokemon and then update the contents in the "Input" tab of Voltorb Freedom! Press "Solve!" to run the solver again and get updated suggestions for cells to flip.
  • Voltorb Freedom can run with known Voltorbs (e.g. if you're running on an emulator and are using the calculator to speed up your games).
For more help or to make suggestions, submit a new "issue" on github.


What is Voltorb Freedom?

Voltorb Freedom is a solver for Voltorb Flip, a Minesweeper-like minigame in Pokemon SoulSilver and HeartGold. Voltorb Flip is a game of chance: for every puzzle, there ~1 Quadrillion (1×1015) possible answers, of which up to Billions or Trillions may be feasible (they satisfy the puzzle's Voltorb counts and Coin sums). Of those, only one is the actual answer to the puzzle in the minigame.

Other Voltorb Flip calculators use basic logic to suggest your next move. What sets Voltorb Freedom apart is that it finds every feasible answer to a given puzzle and gives you the freedom to decide what to do with that information. Would you rather flip the highest-probability cell or choose the one with the greater reward or the one with the lowest chance of having a Voltorb? Other calculators decide for you. Voltorb Freedom lets you choose.
